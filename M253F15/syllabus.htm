Course Content, Topics, and Goals:

This course introduces multi-variable calculus. The textbook is J. Stewart, Calculus, 8th ed. Early Transcendentals, Cengage 2016. We will only cover chapters 12-16. Topics include:
  • vectors,
  • curves and surfaces,
  • vector-valued functions,
  • partial derivatives,
  • gradient/divergence/curl operations,
  • multiple integrals, and
  • integrals along curves/surfaces/solids.
At the end of this course you should be able to apply the tools of single-variable and multi-variable calculus to problems which arise in other subjects, such as in the sciences and engineering.
